# Selected Approach: Modern Minimalist Luxury (Light Theme)

I have selected the **Modern Minimalist Luxury** approach. This aligns perfectly with the user's request for a "light theme as per the logo" and the "premium concierge" positioning. It balances the need for a high-end feel (Playfair Display, Gold Accents) with the requirement for a robust, scalable business system (Clean layouts, clear typography). The light theme will differentiate it from the dark reference site while maintaining the same structural integrity.

**Design Philosophy**: "A Mobile Sanctuary of Light and Luxury."
We will translate the dark, heavy luxury of the reference into a bright, airy, and welcoming premium experience. The gold from the logo will pop beautifully against a cream/white background, and the use of serif fonts will maintain the "high-end" status.
